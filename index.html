Poetry – "Tahir Umar – Dil se likha ek naam"

Tanha raaton mein sapne bunta hai,
chhoti si umar mein bade kaam karta hai.
Kalam se jazbaat likhta hai woh,
aur code mein khwabon ko jeeta hai woh.

Dil se seedha alfaazon ka raasta,
har lafz mein hota hai uska vaasta.
Na sirf likhne ka hunar rakhta hai,
dilon ko chhoo jaaye, aisa asar rakhta hai.

Waqt se pehle sambhal gaya hai,
Tahir Umar ek naya sitaara ban gaya hai.
